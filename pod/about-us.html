Otakunaime started as a hobby project in 2023, when we decided to turn our passion for anime into a business. We wanted to share our artworks with other fans and make some extra income. Since then, we have grown to a team of 10 artists and designers, and we have sold over 10,000 products to customers from all over the world. We are proud to be a part of the anime community, and we strive to uphold the values of creativity, diversity, and excellence in everything we do.
