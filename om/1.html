About US

Tech Solutions

We are TechSolve, a team of dedicated and experienced IT professionals who provide reliable and affordable tech support solutions for businesses of all sizes.

We started TechSolve in 2020, after working for various IT companies and realizing that there was a gap in the market for quality tech support services that cater to the specific needs of each client. We wanted to create a company that would not only fix the technical issues, but also help the clients improve their IT systems and processes, and ultimately, their business performance.

Our mission is to provide personalized, efficient, and cost-effective tech support solutions that help our clients achieve their goals and overcome their challenges. We believe in building long-term relationships with our clients, based on trust, transparency, and mutual respect.

    We offer a range of tech support services, including:
  • Hardware and software installation, maintenance, and troubleshooting
  • Network setup, configuration, and security
  • Data backup and recovery
  • Cloud computing and hosting
  • Web design and development
  • IT consulting and training

We work with various platforms and technologies, such as Windows, Mac, Linux, Android, iOS, Microsoft Office, Google Workspace, WordPress, Shopify, and more.

We are available 24/7, via phone, email, chat, or remote access. We can also visit your premises if needed.

If you are looking for a reliable and affordable tech support partner, look no further than TechSolve. Contact us today for a free consultation and quote.
