About Shoumya Tour and Travels

Welcome to Shoumya Tour and Travels. We offer a wide range of travel options, from adventure tours to luxury destinations.

The majestic mountains of Nepal unfold before you like a surreal painting, each peak telling tales of adventure and spirituality. The breathtaking Himalayan range, including icons like Mount Everest, invites you to witness nature's grandeur at its zenith. Trekking through the picturesque Annapurna and Langtang regions, you'll discover serene villages nestled in the valleys, offering a glimpse into the resilient spirit of the mountain communities.
Masoori became a delightful discovery on my culinary expedition through the Indian subcontinent. The enchanting aroma of Masoori rice lingered in local markets and family kitchens, inviting me to explore its rich flavors. The slender grains, a visual delight, added an elegant touch to traditional dishes, turning each meal into a cultural experience.

Teerth Yatra is a pilgrimage tourism company that offers comfortable, personalized, and diligently planned tours to sacred places in India. They travel in small groups to personalize your experience and take care of senior citizens to give them the best experience. Their knowledge-based yatras and immersive narrations of the guides make you learn a lot about the spiritual destination. They have a team of trained and multi-lingual guides and operation experts led by a yatra director - at your service during the Yatra.
